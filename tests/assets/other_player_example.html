    About Brecht Dejaegere
    

    

    

    Who is Brecht Dejaegere?
    Brecht Dejaegere is 31-years-old, born on 29 May 1991, and is a right-footed professional football player from Belgium. Born in Handzame, Brecht Dejaegere has also played in Jupiler Pro League for AA Gent. He currently plays as a Midfielder, Attacking Midfielder (Centre) in Ligue 1 for club Toulouse.
    How good is Brecht Dejaegere?
    Based on our FC Ranking and match performance analysis he currently has a ranking of
60 out of 99 which is 'Average-level' compared to other football players around the world.
