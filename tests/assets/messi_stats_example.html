    About Lionel Messi
    

    

    

    Who is Lionel Messi?
    Lionel Messi is 35-years-old, born on 24 June 1987, and is a left-footed professional football player from Argentina. Born in Rosario, Santa Fe, Lionel Messi has also played in Supercopa de España for Barcelona. 

    Born
 in 1987, Lionel Messi is regarded as one of the greatest players of the
 modern era. Having moved from Rosario in Argentina to Barcelona's 
famous La Masia facility at the age of 13, Messi was treated for a 
growth hormone deficiency while developing his unprecedented talent 
alongside the likes of Andres Iniesta and Gerard Pique, before making 
his first-team debut at the age of 17 in 2004. From there he developed 
from a fleet-footed left-sided player into one of the most relentless 
goalscorers - and assist providers - in the history of Spanish and 
European football, competing on the grandest stages and striking a 
rivalry - numerically at least - with contemporary Cristiano Ronaldo. 
Messi has broken every conceivable goalscoring record with Barcelona and
 has won four Champions League titles, as well as five Ballons d'Or. 
Quite simply, Messi is a once-in-generation player who still has many 
years ahead of him yet. He currently plays as a Striker, Attacking 
Midfielder (Centre) in Ligue 1 for club PSG.
    How good is Lionel Messi?
    Based on our FC Ranking and match performance analysis he currently has a ranking of
73 out of 99 which is 'Competent-level' compared to other football players around the world.
